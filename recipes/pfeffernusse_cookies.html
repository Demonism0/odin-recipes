Pfeffernusse Cookies

Description

Pfeffernüsse are German Christmas cookies spiced with black pepper, cinnamon, cardamom, and other spices.

"Pfeffernusse" translates to "peppernuts," which makes sense — these cookies are flavored with a cozy blend of warm spices, including black pepper. They also traditionally contain anise.

Ingredients

  • Molasses and honey
  • Shortening and margarine
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugars
  • Anise extract
  • Spices
  • Baking soda
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the first four ingredients on the stove. Cool, then beat in the eggs.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in another bowl, then add the wet mixture. Chill.
  3. Roll the dough into balls.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until firm. Dust with confectioners' sugar.
  5. Allow the cookies to cool completely. Store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to four days.
